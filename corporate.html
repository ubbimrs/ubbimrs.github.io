  • Our Mission/Vission

UBBIM RESOURCES SDN BHD hereinafter known as URSB was incorporated in 2001. Since its inception URSB has grown into a successful and dependable medium sized construction company. URSB also provides total Project and construction management solutions and Design & Built Services to client ranging from government agencies to private and multinationals companies. We have extensive experience across a variety of projects including commercial, industrial and oil & gas. We have earned a reputation for completing projects incorporating professional management techniques and procedures coupled with a dedicated professional project team in ensuring projects are completed with superior quality, within schedule and budget.

The key vision of the Company is to have a strong, dynamic and innovative management, teamwork to improve quality of services and hence commit to excellence. Our design team comprising Architects, engineers and technical specialist consistently search for new innovative ideas and processes including incorporating sustainable construction development practices and green building concept in their design which shall not only protect the environment but bring about long term economic benefits and a better living society.

URSB in line with the pursuit towards excellence, it had obtained the certification of ISO 9001:2000 MS 9001:2000 and in due course shall obtain the OSHA 18001 : 2007 for Safety and Health and EMS ISO 14001 : 2004 for Environment Management System.

URSB strives to deliver additional value to our clients by searching for innovative solutions in planning, design and construction of their project. We foster teamwork, corporate citizenship, safety, social responsibility and integrity as non negotiable elements of our business operations.

OUR VISION is to be a leading Design and Build Contractor providing innovative project management services and state of the art Design and Construction delivery techniques through a highly competitive and efficient business proposition.

OUR MISSION is to continuously improve and add value in our quality or services to our client and community whilst enhancing the development of core competencies and our human capital development.

OUR CORE COMPENTENCY

Our dedicated professional workforce had over the year gained sufficient experience and knowledge to develop innovative business propositions. Today we are proud to have incorporated our experiences as core competencies.
